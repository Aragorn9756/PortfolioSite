Who in Heaven's name is Samus Aran?

For the uninitiated, Samus Aran is A female human bounty hunter in Nintendo's Metroid franchise. A staple from the very beginning of video games, Samus has been around since 1986, in which she debuted as the protagonist of Metroid. For over 30 years she has been fighting space pirates, the pirate queen Mother Brain, the oversized pterasaur Ridley and the titular alien parasites, metroids. In that time she has cultivated an dedicated fanbase who can only wait eagerly and patiently for her next, all too infrequent outing

In this slideshow I would like to introduce you to her ultimate weapon, her power suit. The suit is gift from her foster family, the unimaginably ancient and powerful race of aliens, the Chozo. A modular technological marvel, Samus is constantly improving and upgrading it. Allow me to show you how it's design and functionality has changed throughout the years.
