Welcome!

This site is how I will show what I have learned and the progress I have made, and boy am I excited to show my stuff! Although the subject matter for each page is set, I am a geek at heart and will have all sorts of geeky references throughout my site. For example, the color pallet for the site was inspired by FromSoftware's Dark Souls series, and did you notice the icon on the tab of your browser? I hope you enjoy the personality I've tried to breath into this site and may the odds be ever in your favor.

The End Goal

People are always curious as to what your plan is. "Oh, you're going to school? What degree are you getting? What are you going to do with it?" It's been a long convoluted journey for me, but I think I finally know what I want to do with my time. I would like to help make quality video games. Video games have always been a draw for me. They are, at their best, interactive stories that draw you in like no other medium can. I have always been awed at the work of my favorite developers. It has taken me a while to decide that being a part of that creative process is a viable way for me to provide for my family. It is how I can truly do what I love. And I can't wait to get started!
